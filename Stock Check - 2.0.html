About this Tool

This Batch Stock Lookup tool is built to simplify large-scale product checks. Paste multiple product codes at once, and the system will automatically fetch real-time stock details, validate their availability, and flag invalid or outdated codes.

Results are organized into clear categories — Valid Stock, Invalid/Remarks, Vintage Changes, and Happy to Wait.

In short: faster lookups, fewer errors, and more confidence in your stock data.

If the result shows an error/NA for an item, please double-check it with GV.
